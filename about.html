Colour Buns

The hamburger bread we use at our restaurant is not your typical boring beige color. Our buns come in a variety of colors, each with its own unique natural dyeing ingredient. From bright greens to deep purples, we use only natural food coloring to achieve these vibrant hues.

We believe that using all-natural ingredients is not only better for the environment, but it also makes for a healthier and tastier bun. Our customers can feel good about indulging in a colorful hamburger that is free from artificial colors and preservatives.

So come on in and try one of our delicious hamburgers on a colorful bun!

Toppings

At BYOB, we believe that the quality of our toppings is just as important as the quality of our beef and buns. That's why we use only the freshest, highest-quality toppings in all of our burgers. From crisp lettuce to juicy tomatoes, our toppings are always fresh and bursting with flavor.

We're also proud to say that all of our toppings are sourced from Canadian suppliers. We believe in supporting local farmers and producers, and we're committed to using only the best ingredients in our food.

So when you bite into a burger at BYOB, you can be confident that every element of it is of the highest quality, from the grass-fed beef to the all-natural toppings.

Grass-fed Beef

At our restaurant, we take pride in serving high-quality, grass-fed beef. We believe that the way animals are raised and fed directly affects the quality of the meat. That's why we choose to source our beef from local farms in Canada that prioritize responsible and sustainable farming practices.

Our grass-fed beef is free of antibiotics and added hormones, ensuring that you're getting a natural and healthy meal. The cows are allowed to roam freely, graze on nutrient-rich grass, and live in low-stress environments.

We believe that you deserve the best, and we're committed to providing you with the highest quality beef possible. That's why we're proud to serve grass-fed beef that is not only delicious but also ethically and sustainably sourced. Come try it for yourself and taste the difference!
